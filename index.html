About Me

My name is Ma. Samantha Elizabeth M. Depamaylo, a 20-year-old resident of San Pedro, Laguna. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology with a Specialization in Web and Mobile Applications at FEU Alabang. Alongside my academic pursuits, I have a profound passion for the performing arts, with a particular focus on dance. Having been engaged in dance since childhood, I am now an active member of a dance group where I continue to develop and showcase my creativity, discipline, and expressive capabilities through performance. I consider myself a creative thinker with a strong inclination toward innovative problem-solving. Whether it involves designing user-friendly interfaces, implementing new features, or generating original ideas, I always strive to merge aesthetics with functionality. My ultimate goal is to create digital experiences that are not only efficient and reliable but also visually engaging and impactful for end-users. In addition to my creative and technical interests, I am also passionate about sports, particularly badminton. This sport has instilled in me valuable life skills such as focus, perseverance, and the importance of maintaining both mental and physical discipline. These qualities continue to shape my approach to academic, personal, and professional endeavors. As a student, artist, and athlete, I am committed to continuous growth and excellence in all areas I engage with, aiming to make meaningful contributions in both the technology and creative fields.
